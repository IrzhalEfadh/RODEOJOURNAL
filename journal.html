BEHIND RODEO

love has always been the thing i orbit around when i write songs. but RODEO is different. this time, i wasn’t chasing love, i was soaking in it. fully. finally. not wondering if i was enough. not hoping they’d stay. this album came from the warmth of arms that didn’t let go.

RODEO is about thirst. for touch, yes—but not just skin. it’s about craving presence. eyes that look and keep looking. a hand that lingers. a silence that feels like safety. there’s a sensuality that runs through the whole thing, but it’s not about lust. it’s about wanting to be felt deep, like someone’s memorizing your soul.

it sounds reckless. maybe a little sinful. but honestly, RODEO is the most romantic shit i’ve ever made. it’s messy, it’s bare, it’s loud in all the softest ways. a sinner can still be a good lover. especially when he finally knows what being loved back feels like.
